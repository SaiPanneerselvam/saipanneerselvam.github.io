Class.js help

Install update to update.py

We have got an installer for Class.js! To use the installer, open our github repository, and go to releases. Once you get to releases, take the latest version, and where it says , Right-click. Click copy link and open the update.py (if you don't have it, click on the word update.py at the top. A download will appear, save it.). Where it asks for the link, paste and then click enter. It should automatically download and install the update.
